Ben the Bunny

This project was my final project in my CSE 167: Introduction to Computer Graphics class. Here is the website. The goal of this project was to implement at least two of the effects given to us and to create a story using those two effects using a video. Here is the final video.


The inspiration of my story: Ben the Bunny. Here is the video.

I had to think long and hard about what my story could be about. From previous homework assignments for this class, I already had the bunny object. One day I stumbled across this old video that I recorded of my friend Ben on the birthday of my freshman year. He was acting as a bunny while singing Happy Birthday to me. After watching the video, I realized I could connect these two ideas and make a story from them.


The first part of the story, showing Ben the Bunny.




The effects that I created were the glow effect, bump mapping effect, and the toon shading.

The story that I came up with was: Ben the Bunny has other friends who are at a club together. They are "high" in the sky, and this effect is shown by the sky background, the airplane, and the moon. The music turns on and the strobe lights turn on, making them jump up and down to the music, causing them to glow as they become in an altered state. In the video, the end shows the bunnies jumping as the lights show "CSE 167" glowing.


The final part of the video, paying tribute to my class.

The final product was this story and these effects, and I received an A on this project. This was one of the most difficult projects I've ever had to do. I spent a lot of time on it by myself and had to teach myself a lot of different tasks. This project was a great learning experience for me because I was able to create a story from different pieces, use my imagination, and use my time management skills to create a great final project and result.
